enum GtvIntersect

Status codes returned by GTV functions which check if a vertex inside, outside or on the surface of a simplex, e.g. a tetrahedron.

Enumerator
GTV_ON

GTV_IN

GTV_ON_FACET 

vertex lies strictly inside tetrahedron

GTV_ON_EDGE 

vertex lies on a facet of tetrahedron

GTV_ON_VERTEX 

vertex lies on an edge of tetrahedron
