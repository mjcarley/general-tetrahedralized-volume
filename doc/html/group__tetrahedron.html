Detailed Description

A convenience macro for the quality measure of a tetrahedron. This actually calls gtv_tetrahedron_radius_ratio, although the behaviour may change in future.

gdouble gtv_tetrahedron_quality_radius_ratio ( GtvTetrahedron *  t)

Calculate the radius ratio quality measure for a GtvTetrahedron, as in Proposal of Benchmarks for 3D Unstructured Tetrahedral Mesh Optimization, Julien Dompierre, Paul Labbe, Francois Guibault, Ricardo Camarero, 1998. This is three times the ratio of the inradius and the circumradius of t and will be one for an equilateral tetrahedron.

Parameters
ta GtvTetrahedron.
Returns
the radius ratio quality measure of t.
